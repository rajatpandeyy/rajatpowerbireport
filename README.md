# üìä Power BI Business Analytics Dashboard

This project is an end-to-end interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze business performance across **Sales**, **Marketing**, **Operations**, **Customer Insights**, and **Management KPIs**.  
It uses a unified dataset to provide a complete view of business trends, customer behavior, product performance, and operational efficiency.

---

## - Key Features

### **1. Executive Overview**
- Total Sales: **18.73M**
- Total Profit: **8.71M**
- Total Orders: **2K**
- Total Customers: **493**
- Profit Margin: **0.47**
- Avg Order Value: **9.36K**
- Trends by Year, Quarter, Month, Day, and Region
- Product-level analysis of Sales, Profit, and Stock on Hand

---

### **2. Sales & Marketing**
- Campaign Spend vs Conversions (Scatter Plot)
- ROI and Cost per Conversion by Campaign
- Sales, Avg Order Value, and Profit by Category and Region
- Detailed campaign performance table

---

### **3. Operations**
- Avg Delivery Days trend
- On-Time Delivery % and Target Gauge
- Warehouse-level Stock on Hand and Reorder Levels
- Total Orders by Warehouse and Category

---

### **4. Customer Insights**
- NPS Score, Avg Rating, and Total Reviews
- Total Customers by Region (Pie Chart)
- Customer-level details: ratings, reviews, purchases
- NPS Score by Product

---

### **5. Management View**
- High-level KPIs: Sales, Profit, Profit Margin %, Orders, Customers
- Sales & Profit trend by Month-Year
- Top 3 Product Contribution %
- Sales Rank by Product
- Customer segmentation: Total, Repeat, Loyal
